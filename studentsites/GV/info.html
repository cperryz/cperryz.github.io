Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band who formed in High Green, Sheffield in 2002. The band members include- Alex Turner (lead vocals, rhythm/lead guitar), Matt Helders (drums, vocals), Jamie Cook (lead/rhythm guitar) and Nick O'Malley (bass, backing vocals). The former bassist Andy Nicholson left the band shortly after their debut album was released in 2006.

Currently they have five studio albums- Whatever People Say I Am, That's What I'm Not (2006), Favourite Worst Nightmare (2007), Humbug (2009), Suck It and See (2011) and AM (2013). They have a 6th album rumoured to be released in May 2018, the information for this was given by the drummer Matt Helders in a conversation with a fan but was not confirmed by representatives of the band. The album has since been confirmed to have a release date of May 11th 2018 and the title will be 'Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino'. Their debut album is the fastest-selling debut album by a band in British chart history and is confirmed to be re-issued on vinyl at some stage in 2018.

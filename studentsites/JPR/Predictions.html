These two countries are my pick to get to the final and meet each other, in what should be a quite entertaining match if this is the case.

Germany won the last World cup in 2014 by decimating every team that stood in there path, most memorably the famous 7-1 they gave Brazil in their own backyard.

That was a semi final as well and they made it look so easy and they looked dead on favourites to win the euros as well in 2016 but failed to impress.

With France on the other hand, they had a disappointing world cup campaign in 2014, but in some peoples minds they were favourites to win the Euro's as well as it was hosted by France.

They looked dead on to win that tournament as well by reaching the final with ease, but a lack leadership, quality, composure and concentration, they fell at the final hurdle by being beaten by Portgual 1-0.

Thats in the past now, the focus on this world cup and I predict that these two teams will meet each other in the final, but what team do i think will just edge it?

I think i have to go with France on this one. This is because they showed that they have a great depth in the squad and a youthful team, but its that one question of do to they have what it takes to get them over the finishing line? like we know Germany can.

We can only find out this summer!

Crucial MX500 - Best Budget SATA SSD

Crucial gives you a lot of bang for the buck with the MX500. Don't be fooled by other inexpensive drives with slightly faster specifications in real-world testing, the MX500 consistently outperforms them, with performance just a little bit slower than drives that cost considerably more. It's a value champion, with the entry-level 250GB option costing around $85, and even a full terabyte for less than $300.

Google's first VR Doodle celebrates French illusionist and director Georges Méliès

Google is unveiling its first VR and 360-degree interactive Doodle to commemorate French illusionist and film director Georges Méliès on the anniversary of his celebrated silent film The Conquest of the Pole, which was released on this day in 1912. The Doodle, called "Back to the Moon" is a collaboration between Google Spotlight Stories and Google Arts & Culture.

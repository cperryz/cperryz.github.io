Imaqtpie

Michael "Imaqtpie" hails from Margate, Florida in the USA. He was initially a midlane player and played for a minor team called Oh God Bears in 2011. He then started playing for Team Dignitas in 2012 and finally retired in 2015. Currently, he reguarly streams League of legends on Twitch and is regarded as the most popular and succesful league of legends streamer in the world. According to reports, he makes $2 million a year and has a net worth of around $1m.

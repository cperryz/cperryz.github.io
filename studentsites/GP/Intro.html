How e sports and streaming are taking over the gaming industry

E sports and streaming are the two main methods of making money while playing video games. E-sports are multiplayer video games played competitively for spectators, typically by professional gamers. E-sports are an emerging sport which is attracting more and more viewers, therefore players are starting to earn lots of money. The most popular E-sports game is League of legends, the genre of this game is MOBA which is the main genre of E-sports. There are many live-stream websites dedicated to streaming video-games, the main streaming websites are Twitch, youtube, Azubu etc. Streamers such as Tyler1, Gross gore and Ninja will make money from donations and also advertisements on their channel and on their live-streams. Streamers are popular because they can express their personality to an audience while playing a game that the audience may like- helping them get better at that specific game.

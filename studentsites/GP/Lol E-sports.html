Lol E-sports

E-sports are competitive tournaments of video games, which is played amongst proffesional gamers who will play for a team. The most popular game regarding the E-sports scene is League of legends. The owners, Riot games run the most popular leagues- 'LCS'. These are two leagues in the continents North America and Europe with ten teams per continent. Each annual season is divided into two splits, spring and summer which will conclude with play off tournaments between the top six teams from their respective region. There are also other leagues in Asia which are known as the LCK and LPL. All of the top teams in each leagues will compete in a tournament at the end of the year which is called worlds. This tournement is dominated by the asian teams as they have won 5 of the last 6 world championships.

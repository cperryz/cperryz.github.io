Gross Gore

Gross gore is a controversial league of legends streamer who is well known for only playing the champion, 'Twisted fate'. People enjoy watching him because of his eccentric persionality during his twitch streams- he was diagnosed with bipolar disorder making him at the centre of a lot of drama in the online community. In April 2016, he was permanently banned from Twitch because he accused an E-sports caster of being a "nonce". He was unbanned from twitch in July 2017 because his accusations were actually true. As of recent, he currently streams League of legends and does several IRL streams on twitch.

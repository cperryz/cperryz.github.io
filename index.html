So what is this all about?

I am an IT and Computing teacher at Cheadle College, now part of the Trafford College Group

These pages are to help me practice my html, CSS and javascript skills and to showcase good examples of students work.

We are delivering the Cambridge Technicals at Level 2 and 3 and deliver the units we think are best for our students to access good jobs, university places and other interesting career opportunities.

As part of our work on the CTEC, students have produced game prototypes using Construct 3 software. Students have generated these ideas, designs and coded them.
